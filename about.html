Our Story

It Started with a Lawnmower

Our partnership started with a lawnmower. As teenagers, we built a lawn care business from the ground up—a frontline education in the staggering waste of traditional irrigation. The spark for Pinpoint hit us years later while installing another archaic sprinkler system in my rental property. It was infuriating how little had changed.

After moving to the drought-plagued Southwest regions of the US, the problem came into sharp focus; this wasn't just about waste, it was about necessity. We remained close friends as we began our careers and have formed a bond that has allowed us to make unprecedented progress developing intelligent hardware that Commercial Real Estate needs and wants.

We have the necessary skills and relationships to solve a problem we've understood our entire lives.
